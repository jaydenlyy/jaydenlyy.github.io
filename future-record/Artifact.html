Artifact Analysis: The Telephone
Date: 27052049

Since the dawn of time, humans have been obsessive with their own evolution. Everyday, they work toward one goal, progress and functionality. As a result, it has turned our society into a bleak future, trading peak performance and function for identity and expression. This artifact analysis highlights the journey telephones have taken since it's conception to where it is today, in the Year 2049.
As hard as it might seem to believe, this is what a telephone looked like back in the ancient times. The evolution of the phone has been an expansive journey. What once began as a device of exploration to connect people with others across the world, has since become a booming market of competition and wealth.

Massive corporations gaining power through their domination of this market through innovation and an addictive cycle of consumption through these devices.
