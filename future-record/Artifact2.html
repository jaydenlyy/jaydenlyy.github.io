Artifact Analysis: Artificial Intelligence and Human Interaction
Date: 06062049

Artificial Intelligence acts as an essential part of our daily lives today, as it helps us get up in the morning, sets the coffee, makes us breakfast and so much more. Our reliance on this technology has lead to a global loss of identity and human interaction. We would much rather interact with AI for getting things done than to look to other human beings for help. We trade efficiency and the convenience of AI technology for human connection, robotics have evolved to become so much more effective in the field compared to a regular human being could ever be. I would not be surprised if AI was the next thing to replace us humans completely...
This artifact showcases the first ever Alexa AI created in November of the Year 2014. A compact device that is able to take control of all functions within the house for ease of executing daily tasks for it's user.

It showcases humanity's early innovation and the start of their obsession with progress in replacement for identity. Even 35 years ago, designs by massive corporations featured a loss of identity and strived for hyper minimalism.
