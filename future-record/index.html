FUNCTION AND EFFICIENCY IN EVERYDAY OBJECTS
Global brands and businesses today are continuously focusing on improving their manufacturing process and product to be more efficient, as it will ultimately bring in more money because their product is easier to use and they save on costs. Therefore, on the extreme side of the spectrum, I imagine the future to be stripped of useless ornamentation and add-ons, and will be more minimalistic in design. Lack of identity and replaced with functionality.
